Where Health Matters and Support Begins.

The K Homecare, a NYS DOH licensed home health and care coordination agency was found in 1987 by the Chief Executive Officer, Luis Pons. His vision is to address the healthcare disparities that face the residents of New York. In doing so, the organization has had the ability to make long lasting positive impact on how individuals and families access care in a culturally competent manner. The K Homecare’s guiding principles improve the health and wellbeing of disenfranchised children, families, and individuals. We have established resource networks and relationships that partnered with us in assisting clients. Our expertise is working with families that have been affected by HIV/AIDS, Diabetes, Substance Use, Mental Health Disorders, and Cognitive Issues.
